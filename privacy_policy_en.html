2. What Data is Collected?

2.1 Locally Stored Data

The following data is stored exclusively locally on your device:

  • Meal Plan: Your planned recipes and dates
  • Shopping List: Ingredients and their status (checked/unchecked)
  • Language Settings: Your preferred language (German/English)
  • App Settings: Personal preferences

This data is stored using "Shared Preferences" technology and never leaves your device.

2.2 Camera Access

The app may access your camera to:

  • Take photos for user-submitted recipes
  • Take photos for support requests (e.g., screenshots of issues)

These images are only transmitted when you actively choose to upload them.

2.3 Gusto Backend Server

The app communicates with our backend server (gustoapp.onrender.com) to:

  • Load recipe data (recipe names, ingredients, instructions, images)
  • Perform recipe search and filtering

⚠️ Note on Recipe Submission

When you submit a recipe, the following data is transmitted to and stored on our server: recipe title, ingredients, preparation steps, tags, timing, and optionally a photo. Images are stored with Cloudinary (cloudinary.com). Cloudinary's Privacy Policy also applies.

External Data Processing

2.4 Google Play Integrity API

The app uses the Google Play Integrity API to verify app authenticity and prevent misuse. An integrity token issued by Google is transmitted to our server. Google's Privacy Policy also applies.

3. What Data is NOT Collected?

  • ❌ No personal data (name, email, phone)
  • ❌ No location data
  • ❌ No usage statistics or analytics
  • ❌ No advertising IDs or tracking
  • ❌ No sharing with third parties for advertising

4. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: To load recipe data from the Gusto server and for the Google Play Integrity API
  • Camera (optional): To take photos for recipes or support requests
  • Storage: For saving local data

5. Data Security

Your data is protected by the following measures:

  • Local data (meal plan, shopping list) is stored only on your device
  • No user accounts or login required
  • Encrypted connection (HTTPS) for all external API calls
  • Submitted recipes are stored without being linked to a user identity

6. Your Rights

You have the right at any time to:

  • Access: Know what data is stored
  • Deletion: Delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • Revocation: Revoke permissions in device settings

7. Deleting Your Data

All locally stored data is completely deleted when you:

  • Uninstall the app, or
  • Clear app data in device settings

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy. The current version is always available in the app and online.

9. Children

This app is not specifically designed for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
